About ATNP Database

The ATNP Database is a specialized resource focusing on plant-derived anticancer natural products and their related compounds. It integrates information on compounds, herbs, and spectra, providing tools for search, browsing, and similarity comparison.

Our goal is to support research in natural product chemistry, drug discovery, and the modernization of traditional medicine. By offering comprehensive data and intuitive tools, ATNP facilitates both academic studies and practical applications in pharmaceutical development.

The Compound List section presents all anticancer natural compounds stored in the database. In the compound detail page, three tables are provided: (1) Compound Information, which displays the compound’s general information including name, SMILES, database source, and structure; (2) Herb Sources, listing the potential plants containing this compound, with each herb detail page showing basic plant information and spectrum comparisons; and (3) Similar Compounds in Herbs, presenting plants containing structurally or spectrally similar compounds, together with spectrum comparison details.

The Herb List section displays all plants that contain anticancer natural compounds. By clicking View, users can access the list of anticancer compounds identified in the selected plant.
